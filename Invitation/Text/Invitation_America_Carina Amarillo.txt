Name: Carina Amarillo;
Country: Argentina;
Title: We Are All Part Of The Same;
Year: 2021;
Concept: The theme of new hope, in the context of the current pandemic, opens the possibility of learning, with a hard lesson, that we are all part of the same. Life as a whole, what affects one part affects the whole. Being responsible for our role on the planet and respect for life is a task that begins today and every day.;
