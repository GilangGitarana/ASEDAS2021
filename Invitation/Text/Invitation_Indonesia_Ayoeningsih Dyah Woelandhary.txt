Country: Indonesia;
Title: Nyai Bumi;
Year: 2021;
Concept: the earth is like a child to the motherland (Nyai Bumi), when her child is sick, she will make all the disasters easy, with her affection. It is symbolized by the figure of a mother planting a tree in the middle of a sick world, becoming, to give new hope, so that the earth will recover;
