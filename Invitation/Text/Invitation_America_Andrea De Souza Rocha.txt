Name: Andrea De Souza Rocha;
Country: Uruguay;
Title: "Detachment";
Year: 2019;
Concept: This artworks belongs to my series"curve and opposite curve". In this piece i deal with the detachment of the victim of gender violence, raising herself again with hope, and determination being a refent for victims. The caligraphy of this reminds that..."self estime/ fear for courage/ inner homelessness for shelter etc";
