Title: Con-Fin;

Concept: The series "Con-fin" tries to transmute the feeling of confinement and transform it into a space of freedom. It is trying to get that far imaginary line closer until you can jump over it. It is to go out from a still place to a space without limits. It is trying to find that freedom within me, freedom to create. Freedom to be.;
