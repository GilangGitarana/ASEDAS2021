Name: Ervans Lawuan;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: The Miracle Of The Butterflies;
Year: 2021;
Concept: This artwork dedicated for people who suffering from Covid-19 pandemic to bring their hopes up and to cheer them up. Butterflies are beautiful and also symbolizes hope. I hope that after all of us getting Covid-19 vaccination in the near future, our life will be as beautiful as butterfly.;
