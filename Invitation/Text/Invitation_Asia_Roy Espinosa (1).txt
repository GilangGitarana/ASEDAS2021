Name: Roy Espinosa;
Country: Philipines;
Title: 2 Big Trees (Acrylic On Canvas / 23in X 27in );
Year: 2020;
Concept: Amid this pandemic that brought the world to a halt, the earth started to heal - cleaner air, river and surroundings.
