Title: Wayang Sukuraga;
Year: 2021;
Concept: Wayang is a traditional puppet theatre play that originated from Indonesia. Wayang Sukuraga is a modern philosophical wayang created by Effendi in Sukabumi City, Province of West Java, Indonesia and it has initially been performed to the public since 1997. It is also the only unique modern wayang in Indonesia. The characters of the wayang puppets are taken from parts of bodies, such as, mouth, eye, ear, nose, hand, and foot.;
