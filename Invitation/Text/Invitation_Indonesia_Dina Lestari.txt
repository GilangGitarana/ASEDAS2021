Name: Dina Lestari;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Blockchain Supremacy;
Year: 2021;
Concept: The industrial revolution 4.0 has brought many changes in all aspects such as social, political, economic, health, education, arts, culture, etc. These changes are inevitable, especially due to the threat of the global pandemic which limits human physical activities and replaces them with virtual activities with digital data. Virtual exhibitions with digital works have become a new normal in the world of contemporary art. With the proliferation of digital artwork, the threat of plagiarism practice tends to increase, but over time the world has implemented a new art category known as crypto art. Where works of art have a more secure network with the blockchain cryptocurrency system. This system bringing new hope for the recent and future artists.;
