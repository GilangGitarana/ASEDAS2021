Name: Junaidi Bin Awang;
Country: Malaysia;
Title: A Window Of Hope.;
Year: 2021;
Concept: The window of hope interprets a feeling of an old mother waiting for the return of his estranged children, keep asking of when they will return? the same situation is felt by others who are separated from loved ones during the pandemic.. there are no words and feelings that can describe the hopes of a mother.. a window full of loneliness and new hope. Everyone hopes for something.;
