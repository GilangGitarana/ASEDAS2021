Name: Caitlin Kepler;
Country: United States (USA);
Title: Hope;
Year: 2021;
Concept: The concept for this piece was to pay homage to the frontline workers who worked their butts off to provide care for those infected with Covid-19. Their efforts, along with the scientists who created the vaccine, have made a huge impact on the worlds mentality. That's why I decided to choose a healthcare worker as the face of "hope".;
