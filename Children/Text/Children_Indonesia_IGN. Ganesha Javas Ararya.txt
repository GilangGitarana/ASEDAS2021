Name:IGN. Ganesha Javas Ararya;
Age: 11;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Home And Hope;
Year: 2021;
Concept: This work describes how the home becomes a place to build new hope with new habits to protect oneself and family members during this pandemic. where the role of home and family is very important in breaking the chain of the pandemic. With social distancing, we together maintain hope and family for the sake of all mankind.;
