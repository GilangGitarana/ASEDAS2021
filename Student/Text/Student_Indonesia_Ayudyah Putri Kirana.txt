Name: Ayudyah Putri Kirana;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Hopeful Little Girl;
Year: 2021;
Concept: This pandemic condition is symbolized by the darkness of the room. The little girl in the painting reflects us. The little girl wants to come out to a world that is beautiful and better than the dark room. The sun in the painting represents new hope, and the rainbow symbolizes the beautiful after experiencing bad things.;
