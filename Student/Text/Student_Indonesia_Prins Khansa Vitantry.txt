Name: Prins Khansa Vitantry;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Balance: Harmony;
Year: 2021;
Concept:This painting depicts a hope for a "harmonious freedom" when the Covid-19 pandemic is over. Where "freedom" is denoted as a horse. This "harmony" also describes "happiness and beauty" represented as a ballerina, as well as the radiant natural scenery that symbolizes "peace".;
