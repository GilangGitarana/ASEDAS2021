Name: Azka Zahra Maziya;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: MODRE - Acrylic On Art Paper;
Year: 2021;
Concept: When the pandemic started, when we had to go to school at home, we couldn't go anywhere, Mother became a light at home. Modre (Mother) made us happy and believed that all would be well. She prepares good food, makes the house comfortable. For me, new hope in the adaptation period of new habits (Adaptasi Kebiasaan Baru) is similar to our belief that Mother is Light. And light, though small, if ignited from the heart, can be an eternal hope; for us, for the whole world. --- Ketika pandemi dimulai, saat kami harus sekolah di rumah, tak bisa kemana-mana, Modre (Mother, Ibu) menjadi cahaya di rumah. Dia membuat kami gembira dan percaya bahwa semua akan baik-baik saja. Ibu menyiapkan makanan yang baik, membuat rumah menjadi nyaman. Bagiku, harapan baru di masa adaptasi kebiasaan baru serupa kita percaya bahwa Ibu adalah cahaya. Dan cahaya meski kecil, jika dinyalakan dari hati, ia bisa menjadi harapan abadi; bagi kita, bagi seluruh dunia.;
