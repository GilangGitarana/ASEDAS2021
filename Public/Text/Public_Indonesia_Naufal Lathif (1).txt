Name: Naufal Lathif;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Stay Safe;
Year: 2021;
Concept: The concept of art work entitled stay safe is based on conditions in Indonesia where Covid 19 in Indonesia has not ended even though a vaccine has been found. This poster invites all people to stay safe and take care of their health by wearing masks when leaving the house using the concept of pop colors to be a concern.;
