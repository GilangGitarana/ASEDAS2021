Name: Paramita;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: There Is Always A New Hope (NTT Natural Disaster, April 2021);
Year: 2021;
Concept: The covid-19 pandemic situation, puts a burden and pressure on all humans.  Adaptation to pandemic situations, economic stress, activity restrictions and serious health problems, make it hard for some people to survive.  However, remembering our brothers in NTT who experienced flash floods, extraordinary natural disasters, which brought casualties and materials in a short time, reminds us how lucky we are who did not experience it, and how our humanity is being knocked to reach out to help them in the midst of limitations, because of the pandemic.  So, they can feel a New Hope for survival and start over.;
