Name: Halefi Syifa Ramadhani;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Looking At A Bright Future;
Year: 2021;
Concept: The artwork title of this artwork is “Looking at a Bright Future”. The concept of this artwork is that people of all ages and professions such as doctors, farmers, elementary school children, and others look to the future with bright hopes. This hope arose because of the news about the COVID-19 vaccine.;
