Name: Achmad Afandy;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Pintu Harapan;
Year: 2020;
Concept: Starting from a personal concern,this work tells of a figure as an online driver who was unimaginable at the time of the current pandemic. The figure of the driver hopes that this pandemic will end soon and return to normal by carrying flowers walking towards the door, hoping that behind the door there is a miracle and new hope.;
