Title: Covid 19 | Together We Fight!;
Year: 2021;
Concept: COVID-19 pandemic is a multidimensional phenomenon, including apart from medical aspects, also those resulting from the evolution of civilization. The current outbreak of COVID-19 among Malaysian has reached one figure and so far no improvement abruptly. This artwork explains how the COVID-19 virus enters the human body. It also explains the procedures to prevent and reduce the rate of infection to the surrounding community. Let's fight this COVID 19 virus together!;
