Name: Aulia Wara Arimbi Putri;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Jejak: Karat Biru;
Year: 2021;
Concept: (Revision: additional material information) Along with the changes that exist where the earth is taking a break during the pandemic period, it provides many improvements and restoration for the environment which gives a new hope for the future. This work explores the traces left by iron rust combined with the beauty of indigo dyeing and vinegar on natural cotton fabrics, giving rise to a variety of new colors and shapes that are meant to illustrate the meaning of erasing bad past traces and replacing them in good faith to meet the hope that is more beautiful for our beloved earth;
