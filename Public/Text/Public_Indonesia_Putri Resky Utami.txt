Name: Putri Resky Utami;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Indonesian Culture Against Covid-19;
Year: 2020;
Concept: The work explains that indonesia fought against COVID-19, which was visualized by lumping and kujang horses as an objects representing Indonesian culture from Java island. Kujang has a meaning as a symbol of state sovereignity, while long ago people played kuda lumping to support the war at that time, it can be concluded that the picture was intended to invite people against the corona virus, which was carried out both by government and the community.;
