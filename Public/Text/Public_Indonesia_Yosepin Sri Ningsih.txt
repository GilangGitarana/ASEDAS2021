Name: Yosepin Sri Ningsih;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Habak;
Year: 2021;
Concept: HABAK is a pattern from traditional ikat textile of East Sumba, which is a symbol of hardworking women. The philosophy contained in Sumba textile is equilibrium, where not only men, women also play an important role known as 'Ina Mawolo', namely the mother who spins. In this artwork there is ‘Mamuli’ pattern that symbolizes the womb, Horse that symbolizes honor, and Turtle that symbolizes immortality. Regardless of the limitations, the Sumba people can still produce beauty through their textiles.;
