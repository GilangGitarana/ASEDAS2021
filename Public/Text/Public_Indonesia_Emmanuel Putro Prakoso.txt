Name: Emmanuel Putro Prakoso;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: In Harmony;
Year: 2021;
Concept: This work is entitled "In harmony", a work that describes serenity above an uncertain era. serenity in waiting for a beautiful hope in these limited times. the harmony of life that is deliberately raised to provide motivation to keep hope in any condition and situation. The color circles are an expression of new hope that will wash down the chaos of this pandemic's life.;
