Name: Naufal Lathif;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: A New Hope;
Year: 2021;
Concept: The concept of art work, entitled a new hope, is based on conditions in Indonesia where in Covid 19, there are other disasters such as forest fires and floods, which I described with 2 doors that contained fires and floods, and there were 3 people wearing masks. to invite us all to be strong in the conditions that we are today.;
