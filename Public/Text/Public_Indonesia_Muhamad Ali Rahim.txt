Name: Muhamad Ali Rahim;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Greenyglitch;
Year: 2021;
Concept: This work is made using digital editing techniques from a drawing using a pen and acrylic colors. The narrative idea is an ecosystem hybridity that grows resiliently on the one hand, but constantly gets disturbed on the other. This work, entitled "Greenyglitch", depicts the transformation of a sturdy tree into a flying bird towards its freedom.;
