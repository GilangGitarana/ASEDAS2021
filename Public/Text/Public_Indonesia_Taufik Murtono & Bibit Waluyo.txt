Title: There Is Always Hope 2;

Concept: Epidemics and natural phenomena such as solar eclipses in the concept of ancient civilizations are always associated with mystical events. Apart from being part of mystical beliefs, pandemics are also seen as the effect of an imbalance in the relationship between humans and nature. But the ancient people were always been positive. They believe in a supernatural power that will intervene to solve all problems. They believe there is no a single life hardships which cannot be solved.;
