Name: Drs. I Made Bendi Yudha, M.Sn.;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Ibu Semesta;
Year: 2019;
Concept: Human selfishness makes him always feel dissatisfied, even though he has to destroy the natural resources that have given him the blessing of life. The figure of a mother who is stretched and helpless, is a symbol of this motherland which has been undermined by greedy ants who are competing to gain profit by eating away at existing natural resources, these ants are a symbol of greedy human beings in the millennial era. often forget about the devastating effects they have done to the universe.;
