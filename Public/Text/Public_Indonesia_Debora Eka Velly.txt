Name: Debora Eka Velly;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: A Hope Of Life;
Year: 2021;
Concept: A Hope of Life. The olive tree is a symbol of life and peace. Being in the middle has the meaning of being the center and source of life. There is a dove carrying an olive tree stalk which shows that there is still new hope for a better life. In this work, it is combined with batik that representing characteristic of Indonesian;
