Name: Fatih Jagad Raya;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Coronaism;
Year: 2021;
Concept: In this era, all artists in the creative process are confined by the corona pandemic situation, this is like being led by art actors to make works about corona, almost in all exhibition events, both national and international, almost have the theme of corona. Whatever the style of the artist used in a work must contain a corona element in it.
