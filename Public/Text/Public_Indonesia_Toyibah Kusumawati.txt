Name: Toyibah Kusumawati;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Smile Of Happy Relief;
Year: 2021;
Concept: Most people expect the Covid-19 pandemic to end so that they can resume their normal lives and associate with one another without fear of catching the virus. All will be happy, and the Covid-19 pandemic will be nothing more than a story or a memory. This artwork is made of cowhide, which is processed with chisel, pyrography, and collage techniques.;
