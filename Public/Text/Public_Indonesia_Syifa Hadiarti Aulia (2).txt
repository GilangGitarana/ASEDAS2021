Name: Syifa Hadiarti Aulia;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Heal The World, Sunshine;
Year: 2021;
Concept: "Heal the World, Sunshine" illustrates the differences between two environmental conditions during the COVID-19 pandemic and normal conditions. This artwork tell about a girl’s hopes for the pandemic situation, hope that everything return to happiness like in the past when humans lived freely without being restricted by the covid-19 virus.;
