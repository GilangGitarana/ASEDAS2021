Concept: “Halaman Baru” is Digital Imaging which tries to describe what humans experienced when the 2019 pandemic occurred, where at the beginning of the pandemic humans were shrouded in great fear and anxiety. But all events in this world are the same as a word order on a book page where sometimes a sentence will be written on a new page, regardless of whether the sentence is a continuation of the previous page or a new sentence, humans just have to continue to adapt and make changes for the sake of a distant future. better. Likewise with the “NEW HOPE” concept where we as humans must be able to make a new, better page, and make the previous page an important lesson.;
