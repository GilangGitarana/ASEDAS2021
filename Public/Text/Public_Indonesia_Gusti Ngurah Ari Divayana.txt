Name: Gusti Ngurah Ari Divayana;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Hope The Light That Shines In The Darkness;
Year: 2021;
Concept: This work depicts an angel of death who does not come to take the life of a human child. but gave a glimmer of hope. it is a metaphor for the pandemic sweeping the world. A pandemic on the other hand does not only present fear, but a pandemic is able to present new ways and hopes for human life.;
