Name: Happy Yugo Prasetiya;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Farewell Virus;
Year: 2021;
Concept: This work signifies a hope for human readiness to face the virus. The danger of a pandemic will continue to exist and will always be repeated as it has been recorded in history. The word "farewell" serves as a reminder to us humans to be better prepared to face other viruses in the future. ;
