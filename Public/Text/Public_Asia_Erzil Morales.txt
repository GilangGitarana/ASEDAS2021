Name: Erzil Morales;
Country: Philipines;
Title: “In Harmony Ii”;
Year: 2021;
Concept: This paint represents the fusion of colors in varying shades that can create harmony once combined. Similarly, we are all created different, with different background in life, culture, characteristics but if we get along and try to support one another it will surely create the same harmony. This I hope to witness that we sustain specially in our current pandemic situation. Let us show our love and support to one another;
