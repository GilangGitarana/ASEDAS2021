Name: Ejup Deari;
Country: Netherlands;
Title: The Meteor Of Hope;
Year: 2020;
Concept: This work is part of the theme " New Beginning" I did in 2016. It is about the fact that after a difficult end, comes an easy new start. This work presents a troubled sky, where a meteor of hope falls down. With the message that, as long as there is life, is also hope!;
