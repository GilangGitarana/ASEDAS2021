Name: Ahmad Rofiul Azmi;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Don't Be Affraid;
Year: 2021;
Concept: The corona pandemic is a new problem throughout the world, almost all sectors, be it economic, political, social, are all affected by this outbreak. In anticipating this, many policies have been taken, ranging from large-scale social restrictions to vaccine production and mass distribution. Rooted from this, in this work the writer wants to convey that we don't be afraid of the many changes that will occur and that we don't lose to this pandemic. Depicted with astronauts riding a surfboard preparing to prevent the arrival of a meteor carrying the virus. The authors hope that they will always be vigilant and not be afraid and lose from this pandemic.;
