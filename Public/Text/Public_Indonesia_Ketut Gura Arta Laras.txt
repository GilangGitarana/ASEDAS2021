Name: Ketut Gura Arta Laras;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Rust Streaks;
Year: 2020;
Concept: In the process of artistic creativity, art object material is not always beautiful. The phenomenon of iron waste that is around us presents interesting visuals and characters. Collage is used in the visualization process of this photographic work. The attachment of objects to the photo adjusts to the size of the object itself.;
