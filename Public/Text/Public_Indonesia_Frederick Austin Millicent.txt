Title: "The Tree Is Still Survives To Live";
Year: 2021;
Concept: Pandemic Covid-19 is still ongoing. We have to survive to get a better life in this pandemic or after this pandemic. I made some 3D modeling become a portrait poster, there's a tree surrounded by a Covid-19 virus. The tree is describes us, who survives in this pandemic. The tree want to grow up and be bigger. The virus is go down and will disappear. Just like us, we want get a better life in this pandemic and hope can be better after this pandemic. ;
