Name: Sri Rahayu Saptawati;
Country: Indonesia;
Title: Living Side By Side With Covid;
Year: 2021;
Concept: The painting that I made this time was inspired by a true story experienced by all humans in the world ... The emergence of the plague that has attacked all over the world makes people have to accept and surrender to the situation to stay at home ...It is at home that creativity is created and a new love for the figure of a beautiful ornamental plant with the name aglaonema appears ... To fill the days full of hope, bright colors to welcome joy and as an original form.This batik painting is filled with cheerful colors and gradations of bright colors to give a cheerful impression.;
