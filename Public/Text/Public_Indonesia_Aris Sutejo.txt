Country: Indonesia;
Title: Pandemic Solidarity;
Year: 2021;
Concept: The government has done various efforts to control the Covid-19 pandemic, and one of it being the implementation of social restrictions. Maintaining distance does not mean reducing the values of the cultural solidarity tradition that have become the character of the Indonesian people. Such solidarity act nowadays can still be done using the information and communication technology such as gadget, and it became the best and fastest alternative during the Covid-19 pandemic.;
