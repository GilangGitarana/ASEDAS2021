Title: Cardboard Aesthetic Elements With Batik Pattern For Junior Highschool Students;
Year: 2021;
Concept: online craft workshop provide a challenge in the instructional technique. However both lectures and students quickly adapt resulted in a marvelous and creative works. The topic in this workshop is cardboard recycling with batik pattern, as cardboard waste increase as a result of the escalation of online shopping in pandemi era. This online workshop is intended to raise awareness for students to recycle and have a softskill activities other than using gadget;
